to the gods we must be atoms to the atoms we must be gods

An instance of reflection
Seizes my attention
An optical illusion gazes in my direction
To the creature I feel no connection
The confrontation results in projection

A glimmer past the vanity
All I can ensure of reality
The only guarantee of divinity
I feel my body explicitly

Eerie and self intimate
The borders of my sanity
A limit to the infinite
Yet all that a god can be

I feel in my breath
The strength of the heft
My diaphragm carries the load on my chest

It is all but it is empty
It is relief but it is heavy
Liberation at the ready
Desperation to be steady

Paralyzed by the depth of my consciousness
Physically severed at the base
I am improving my combat against the feeling of material delay

I fear that this feeling is more than I can take
Each time gets harder to return from this state
The further I go the narrower my escape
But around my body I have a life to maintain

So I fold up the vastness
And pack it away
Force my eyes to dilate
There are a few hours left of today
